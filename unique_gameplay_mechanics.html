The Currency System: Blood Gems and Soul Shards

In VampirismMC, traditional gold and emeralds take a backseat to our unique currencies: Blood Gems and Soul Shards. These rare and valuable items serve as the backbone of our economy.

  • Blood Gems: These crimson jewels can be earned through quests, combat, and special events. They're used to purchase rare items, cosmetics, and even special abilities.
  • Soul Shards: Even rarer than Blood Gems, Soul Shards are used for high-level enchantments and to acquire the most powerful items in the game.
